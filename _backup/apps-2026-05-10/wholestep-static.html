Whole is the master table of flove. It orders all of reality into four columns: Substance (what reality is made of), Field (which discipline studies it), Paradigm (the theoretical-poetic lens of flove) and App (the playable practice).

The four questions (metaphysics)

X · WHY · WHAT · HOW — the logical ground that holds up the table.

Before matter, there are four questions that order everything else: X (the simple before the word), WHY (the first intention), WHAT (what exists as such) and HOW (how it relates to everything else).

Remove one and the others collapse. Each is resolved by its own paradigm and practiced through its app.

Mnemonic: X · WHY · WHAT · HOW — the prior, the reason, the thing and the relation.

Biology: from macromolecule to body

From the threshold of life (macromolecule) to the whole body as a network of bi-coupled systems.

From the threshold of life — the macromolecule, which already has memory — to the body as a network of bi-coupled systems. A membrane decides what enters and what leaves: that's where life begins. Cells form tissues, tissues form organs, and organs form systems that sustain each other.

Complexity isn't measured just in parts, but in entanglement between parts. Life and death as a pair, not as opposites.

PracSys trains those links: seeing the body as networks, not as loose pieces.

Linguistics: from sound to sense

From phoneme to seme, from seme to syntax, from syntax to knowledge.

The organism opens its mouth and language appears. Flove maps it as a ladder: from phoneme to seme, from seme to syntax, from syntax to knowledge. Each rung has its app.

Trick: phonology is the system, phonetics is the concrete sound, semantics is what it means, morphosyntax is how it combines, epistemology is how we take it as true.

Closing: language is body becoming bond. No organism, no speech; no speech, no society.

Psychosocial: from psyche to polis

Psychology, sociology, media, law, politics, military — and, as a bridge, economy.

When language settles into bonds, psychology appears; when bonds settle into structures, society appears. Here flove adds media, law, politics and the military as distinct layers of the same fabric — and the economy as a bridge to the final step.

Pay attention to the LowAI paradigm: low-tech principles applied to AI — the project's ethical stance against the noise of brilliant artificial intelligence.

Connection: psychology without sociology is solipsism; sociology without psychology is social engineering. The Economy row below is the bridge to the final step.
