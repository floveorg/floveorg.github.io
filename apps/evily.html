Evily — the dark twin of Crumbler. Sign, in advance, what someone is allowed to do against you, on what scope, and what compensation may put it right.

An Evily is not permission to be hurt — it's a way to name the worst that can happen and bind it to terms, so its sting is already paid for.

Type a name. Tick what you allow them to do. Set the time, the visibility, the compensation. The form's Resume rewrites itself live as you go.

When you're ready, copy it, save it, or publish it. The fire below grows with each line you sign; the water comes when you take one back.
