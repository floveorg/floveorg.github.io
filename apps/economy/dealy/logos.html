Dealy — deal logos

One deal, three states. Every mark carries the same idea — a pivot where something passes between two hands — and moves through the same Open → Agreed → Sealed logic. Switch the state to see them all turn together; hover any mark to seal the deal.

Deal state

Open — the offer is on the table (dashed reach, hollow pivot) · Agreed — the two sides meet (solid) · Sealed — the pivot fills and the mark glows.

Three takes on Dealy

Same pivot, same deal-state logic — three minimal readings of what a deal is: a handoff, a balance, a clasp. Pick one as the brand mark.

Handoff Two arrows cross the pivot — give and get.
Balance A beam tips on the pivot — a fair exchange.
Clasp Two links lock at the pivot — a bond made.

The six subs

Each sub keeps the pivot and the deal-state logic, then adds one minimal idea of its own. Colour names the sub; the glyph keeps the family.

Inventary A crate — what you already hold.
Wanty A reach upward — what you want.
Dealy Handoff + balance — the full deal.
Freed One way out — a gift, freely given.
Shareful A loop back — lent, and returned.
Rewardy A burst from the pivot — what comes back.
