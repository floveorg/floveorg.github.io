Welcome to Wanty ✦

A small tree of essences — six parent wants (Like, Wish, Interest, Purpose, Project, Want), each opening into its own subtabs. Tap a parent to reveal what nests under it, pick a field, write or speak, and watch your legible phrase build itself in real time.

Open the compass display menu and tap ➕ to grow the tree — expand any branch and add your own wants at any depth. Copy, share or publish when you're ready.

Tip: tap the microphone in any field to add a spoken nuance.
