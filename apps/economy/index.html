Point at a coin to read what it does.Tap a coin to open it. Every tool works on its own, and stores what you make on your own machine.

ring onewhat you hold

  • WoWorthing Put a value on something, and say why it holds.
  • WaWanty Write what you want until it reads clearly, from a like to a project.
  • InInventary List what you have, and what each thing is for.

ring twowhat changes hands

  • FrFreed Give something away, on the record. A gift, declared.
  • ShShareful Lend something, with the conditions written down before it leaves.
  • KsKindSwap Swap one thing for another, in a single screen.
  • DeDealy Gift, lend or exchange things with your neighborhood.
  • ·Dealy tiers Change how much of Dealy you see, from KindSwap up to the full offer.

ring threewhat it adds up to

  • ReRewardy Compare the offers you were made, and reach the people behind them.
  • CrCrafty Make something together and split the credit fairly.
  • LoLowy An almanac of living with less: low tech, low consumption, open blueprints.
