About Offer

Offer is the flove space for putting a thing into your neighborhood's hands — as a gift, a lend or an exchange.

Pick a mode below to begin. Each mode opens its own questions — what you offer, where it is, the time and conditions around it — and the summary builds itself as you go.

Tap for to note why you're offering, and tap Offer any time to reopen this note.
