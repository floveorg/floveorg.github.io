    About lowy

    lowy gathers a family of movements — simple living, low tech, low consumption, LowAI, and their roots and practices — into one calm place. It links them, offers an open pack of survival-and-self-reliance blueprints in the spirit of the Global Village Construction Set, and hands out small, practical advice for living more simply.

    It is built the way it argues you should build: one small file, no tracking, no external calls, readable and repairable, working offline and printable on paper. It is part of flove.
