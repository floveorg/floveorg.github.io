About lowy

lowy gathers a family of movements — simple living, low tech, low consumption, LowAI, and their roots and practices — into one calm place. It links them, offers an open pack of survival-and-self-reliance blueprints in the spirit of the Global Village Construction Set, and hands out the small everyday culture of living more simply.

It is built the way it argues you should build: one small file, no tracking, no external calls, readable and repairable, working offline and printable on paper. It is part of flove; sibling to LowAI.

Welcome to lowy

One idea — a good life on less:

  1. Lowy opens by default. Pick any category in the left column to switch — you can light up several at once.
  2. The Sections tabs — Values, Tools, Culture — choose what each shows below.
  3. Tick tools for your pack, keep the sayings you'll live by, propose your own.

Everything stays on your device unless you choose to propose it. Nothing is tracked.
