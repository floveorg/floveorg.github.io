The questions without a floor

Four small tools for the oldest questions — is there something above us, what is good, what is a soul, is the will free. Each runs alone, offline, on your own device. Nothing you answer here leaves it.

There's no hub and no order: the four hang in the same sky, each its own light. Pick one and sit with it. None of them resolves — that is the point.
