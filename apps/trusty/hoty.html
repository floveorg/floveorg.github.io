Hoty — Adore You: Sensorility Requests Assistant. Polite, playful and formal — compose a request you'd want someone to receive, then send it only if it still feels right.

Hoty composes a tender sensorility request piece by piece — opener, body part, tone, mode of touch, incentives and a closing flourish. Walk down the form; each section adds a layer. Your readable message composes itself at the bottom, ready to forward only when both you and the moment feel right.

Disclaimer: requests and agreements are to be kept secret unless authorised by both parts. We encourage adored ones not to be offended by adorers; if any harassment is felt, communitarian mediation services specialised in these situations are available.
