The people around you

Eight small tools for the bonds between people. Each runs alone, offline, on your own device. Nothing you write here leaves it.

They sit in a ring around you, each on its own thread — because a bond is one thread at a time. Follow them clockwise and they tell the arc a bond travels: you trust, it breaks, you agree again, you name the harm, you are tender, you meet, you raise, you live among.

Part of flove · built to work offline, on paper, and without asking anything of you.

Eight threads, one cloth.
