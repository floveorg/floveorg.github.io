The people around you

Eight small tools for the bonds between people. Each runs alone, offline, on your own device. Nothing you write here leaves it.

Trusty sits at the hub, because every other tool here is a mode of trust — trust given, trust broken, trust renegotiated. The seven hang off it, each on its own thread. Follow them clockwise and they tell the arc a bond travels once trust is given: it breaks, you agree again, you name the harm, you are tender, you meet, you raise, you live among.
