Practise the systems

The eleven biological systems from souls, laid out as a living body you arrange by hand.

  1. Tap the Systems card on the left to fan the eleven systems open (five up, five down, alphabetical).
  2. Click a system to drop it into the next free placeholder — or drag it where you want. Click a placed one to send it back.
  3. When a whole row is full, write a reason for it. Save it, delete it, add another below.
  4. Once all eleven are placed, reveal the subsystems: four nervous (right) and four immune (left). Place each on a different system to thread a labelled line across the body.
