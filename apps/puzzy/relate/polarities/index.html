What is polarity balancing?¿Qué es equilibrar polaridades?

Many ideas carry a pair (formal/informal, abstract/concrete, inner/outer…). When you pick one, Polarities invites you to also cover its complement so your note is more robust and fair. Inspired by systemic thinking…and by Laozi. Muchas ideas llevan un par (formal/informal, abstracto/concreto, interior/exterior…).
