What is Souls?

An old projective game in five layers. An animal, a colour, a species you'd inhabit, a sense you'd feel through, and down further — into matter, particles and immune subsystems. Five doors. Same pace, your words.

The portrait writes itself.

    ✺

    Welcome to Souls.

    A small mirror, taken in breaths.

    Old projective games invite the soul to speak through animals, colours, senses, and gestures. Souls is a quiet walk through that mirror — at your pace, in your own words.

    ✦

    Five layers. Each one a door.

    You start with an animal and a colour. From there you go deeper — a species, a sense, the matter you're made of, the particle inside it. Stop whenever it feels enough. Nothing is lost.

    ❉
