About keys

keys is a small ritual for ordering ideas. Ten decks lie face down — five triads (3 cards each) and five pentads (5 cards). You flip one, order its cards however you feel they should go, and (if you wish) record a short why behind that order.

There is no right answer. The keys belong to you: the order you choose and the reason you give become the artifact.

Choose your start

Step 0 is an optional warm-up where you group 15 cards into 5 triads and order them by abstraction. You can skip it and go straight to Step 1.
