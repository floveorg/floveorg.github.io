HackBoth — name what you dislike and what you like in the same breath. The hack lives where they meet.

Every dislike is a like turned up too loud. Every like is a dislike that found its measure. HackBoth invites you to hold both sides at once, until the seam between them shows.

Type a name. Then three things you dislike about them, and three things you like — same breath. The form unfolds row by row.

When you're ready, ask for the hack. Insights, Creative, and Copy come first. The save and share tools follow a little after.
