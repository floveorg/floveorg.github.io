## 1. Where Science sits in the whole

In flove's master taxonomy, Science is the *first instantiation* of the
metaphysical triad WHY/WHAT/HOW into the empirical world. It immediately
fractals into Physics → Chemistry → Biology, each one repeating the same
WHY-WHAT-HOW internally.

```
METAPHYSICS  →  SCIENCE  →  PHYSICS    CHEMISTRY    BIOLOGY
                            (WHY)      (WHAT)       (HOW)
```

The same dimensional language used in metaphysics returns here as concrete
forces, particles, bosons, units and states.

## 2. Dimensions — the `2^N` proposal

Flove's distinctive claim about physics is dimensional:

- The fundamental skeleton is **`2 powered N`**, not Euclidean 3D.
- Base: `2¹` = a relation (length).
- `2²` adds **height** → 2D plane.
- `2³` adds **width** → only here we get Euclidean space, but flove
  insists it is *not* a fundamental 3rd dimension; it's a confluence of
  the 2¹ and 2² limits.
- Adding **time** as another power yields `2⁴ → 2⁸`, reaching what the
  deck calls "8D+ in infinite spacetime."
- Past/future map onto micro/macro, length/height onto micro/macro of
  space — the diagrams in slides §SCIENCE recurse this for several
  pages.

Slogan from the deck:

> *"In (in)finite powered timespace, there is no 3D, nor 5D (but 8D+ in
> infinite). Only back to 2D."*

Operational consequence: flove rejects the popular 3D-of-space framing
as a fundamental coordinate system and prefers a **2-powered relational
geometry** in which width is derived, not primary.

## 3. Forces, Particles, Bosons — the bipolar table

The deck builds a 2×2 of forces and their carriers:

| | Length-axis (micro) | Height-axis (macro) |
|---|---|---|
| **Force** | Gravity | Electromagnetism |
| **Boson** | Higgs | Foton |
| **Force (nuclear)** | Nuclear Strong | Nuclear Weak |
| **Particle/Carrier** | Nucleon, Gluon | Lepton, Weak Boson |
| **Unit example** | Proton | Electron |
| **State** | Water (Earth-Phonon) | Earth (?) |
| **Sense** | Touch | View |

Senses are mapped onto the same axes — **Touch / Taste / Smell / Hear /
View** are placed as the human side of the same dimensional bipoles. This
is one of flove's more idiosyncratic moves: senses are not separate from
physics, they are macro-level organs of the same `2^N` field.

## 4. Chemistry — `2 X^n` shells, valences as dimensions

Flove summarises chemistry with a tight formula:

> *Chemistry = `2 X^n`* where X is the "prefered valence" and n the
> shell index, with `2n²` as the shell capacity limit and 7 the shell
> limit, and `2n` as the valence limit (preferably 3).

A speculative move: **valences as dimensions**. Carbon's 4 valences are
read as a 4-axis dimensional capacity, and Silicon (also valence 4) as
the *outer pole* of carbon — silicon transduces, carbon transforms.

> *"Transductive Silicon 4 ↔ Transformative Carbon 4"*

This sets up the bridge to biology: life is the place where carbon's
transformative valence cycles with silicon's transductive valence — the
deck flags this as a possible "carbon centroidal function" inside
tissue. The numerology `26 − 35 = −9 / 2 = −4.5` is offered as a hint
but explicitly marked speculative.

## 5. Cosmoginies — bipolar cosmologies

`Cosmoginy.m4a` (in the MATHYLOGIC folder) and the deck's *Cosmosystems*
diagram propose multiple cosmologies as confluent variants:

- Standard / Big Bang
- Steady-state
- Cyclical / multiverse (Many-Worlds)
- Pandeist / panentheist

Flove's stance: **all are partial and bipolar**; the confluentist read
treats them as projections of the same `micro↔macro · past↔future`
fractal at different scales of observation.

## 6. Intuitionism — the math-logic feeder for science

`Intuitionism25.10.odt` (3,175 lines) is the longest mathylogic source.
Its key positions, as relevant to science:

- **Constructive over classical**: math statements are true when
  *constructible*, not when their negation fails.
- **Analogy as inference**: the master operator is *analogizing*
  (`Analogizar.mp3`). Hypotheses are analogies between a small known
  case and a large unknown one.
- **Measurement is a sense, not a god**: the measurement problem is
  read as evidence that the observer is part of the observed — no
  amount of refinement makes it disappear, because it's bipolar.
- **Numbers as relations, not entities**: `Uno.mp3`/`VerMedir.mp3`/
  `Line.mp3` express this in poem form. One is the smallest *relation
  in itself*, not a primitive entity.
