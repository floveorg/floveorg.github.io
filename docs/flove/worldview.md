## 1. What flove is

**flove = flow + love.** The site's own browser title is literally:
*"Flove.org – flow love flove"*.

Flove.org is an **integrative project** — a worldview, a documentation
archive, and a constellation of small client-side apps. It tries to
weave together **science, philosophy, linguistics, biology, psychology
and society** under a single relational vocabulary, and then express
that vocabulary as **playable little experiences** (the apps).

The official self-description captures the flavor:

> *"In the more Simplex place there are varying eachs as equilibred
> confluences of a micro & macro and-or past & future and else
> relational analogies to zygot that mimic genesis better. … Love is who
> better links all we've seen and will see getting broader from a simpler
> thing. So… what is in your love? More acts for love. Feed back the source."*

In plain language: every level of reality (atoms, cells, people,
societies) is a **confluence** of complementary opposites; **love** is
the relational name for whatever holds those confluences together; the
project is the practice of mapping that and then *acting on it*.

The name is also a stance: **slow it, flow it, love it** — low-tech,
relational, gift-economic, decentralized, no-flashy-AI.

## 2. The umbrella: ✺ asterism

The eight-pointed asterism `✺` is the brand mark used across the family
(and the shared flove-bar). A second motif is **the genesiacal pair /
cyclon**: any whole is displayed as two complements (subject/object,
micro/macro, give/take, real/realyn, life/death). Many apps lean on this
pattern.

## 4. UX patterns harvested across the family

Small, smart ideas that recur and define the family's usability identity:

- **Tabs / reveals without JS** — radio/checkbox + `:has()` / sibling selectors.
- **Live as-you-type filtering.**
- **Speech-to-text mic buttons** wherever there is a textarea (Web Speech API).
- **Heart-toggle** to mark "this counts".
- **Reward chips** (★ merit, ⛁ resources, € money) — the gift-economy vocabulary.
- **Native share + clipboard** as the default action bar.
- **Magic combine** — pick N items, get a generated phrase.
- **Wizard `ⓘ` info popups** for inline context.
- **Live "global selected" footer** — a persistent strip showing the current choices.

These are framed by a shared header/footer (`.flove-bar`), not replaced.
