# Flove · Poems / Poem-Songs

Manifest of the audio-poems (mp3) and audio-narrations from the FloveAll
archive. The "poem" in flove is a short musical-vocal piece that distills a
metaphysical, ontological or mathylogic concept into singable form. They are
the *poetic* register of the same content the papers and slides express in
prose/diagram. Treat the lyrics-as-concept: every track name doubles as a
keyword in the flove ontology.

> Audio files are not duplicated here (saves space). Paths are absolute on
> the local archive. Convert to text via Whisper if transcripts are needed
> for AI training.

## How to use these in AI training

- Each filename is a flove **lemma**. Treat it as a label.
- The lyrics (when transcribed) are micro-glosses of the lemma.
- Pair each poem with the corresponding paper section and slides for
  multimodal embedding (text + audio + diagram).
- Suggested flow: Whisper → align to paper paragraphs → embed both with
  the same metadata vector for the lemma.
