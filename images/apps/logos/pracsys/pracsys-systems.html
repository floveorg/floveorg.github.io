pracsys — the eleven systems, two designs each

Replacements for the emoji that currently stand in for pracsys's eleven biological systems. Each system gets two candidate icons drawn in the pracsys mark language — cyan line-art, round strokes, node accents — so they sit beside the app logo as a family. Design A is the anatomically literal read; Design B is a more emblematic take that echoes today's emoji. Every icon is pure SVG on a 64×64 grid and holds down to 16 px.

How to read this. Each card shows a system with Design A (left) and Design B (right) at icon scale, plus a 16 / 24 px legibility proof for each. Icons lead with pracsys's cyan; a second hue appears only where it carries meaning — coral for blood & hormones, green for the immune & living threads, purple for the nervous system.
