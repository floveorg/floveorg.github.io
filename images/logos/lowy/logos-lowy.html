logos · lowy — ten marks, and the icon set for its tabs and categories

Every mark is CSS on live text — no images, no web fonts, no external calls. They use lowy's own palette (paper, ink, clay, leaf, gold) and its serif, so any of them drops straight into apps/economy/lowy.html as the .brand. Each animation loops slowly and stops entirely under prefers-reduced-motion.
