souls — nine possible marks

Nine candidate logos for the app that draws a soul portrait from a handful of projective prompts — an animal, a colour, a species — lifting veil after veil toward the self underneath. Each mark is pure SVG, holds down to 16 px, and carries a small motion of its own.

How to read this. Each card shows the mark at logo scale, a small wordmark lockup, and a favicon proof at 16 / 24 / 44 px. Marks lead with souls' mint green; a warm amber marks the soul-core (the light you're looking for), and a dusk purple appears only where a second layer earns it.
