pracsys — ten possible marks

Ten candidate logos for the app that lays the eleven biological systems onto a board as a living body — paired rows around a central spine, threaded with reasons, verbs and immune subsystems. Every mark is pure SVG and holds down to 16 px.

How to read this. Each card shows the mark at logo scale, a small wordmark lockup, and a favicon proof at 16 / 24 / 44 px. Marks lead with pracsys's cyan; a second hue appears only where it carries meaning — coral for the centroid/spine, green for the immune thread, purple for the nerve pairs.
